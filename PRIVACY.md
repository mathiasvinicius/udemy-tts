# Privacy Policy - Udemy Transcript TTS (Free)

Last updated: March 6, 2026

## Data collection

Udemy Transcript TTS (Free) does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

## Data processing

- The extension reads subtitle/transcript text only to perform local text-to-speech.
- Processing happens locally in the browser runtime.
- The extension does not communicate with any remote server.

## Permissions rationale

- `storage`: saves your extension settings (voice, speed, volume, source mode).
- `https://*.udemy.com/*`: required to access subtitles on Udemy lesson pages.
